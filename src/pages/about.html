About Me

I am an undergraduate student at the University of California, Irvine pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science with a specialization in Information. Through my projects, I have developed an interest in creating apps compatible for Android and iOS using React Native. With the abstraction afforded by a single code base, React allows me to focus on developing screens that adopt responsive design and don't compromise a user's experience.

Though I am still new to developing user interfaces, I strive to expand my knowledge and skillset through taking relevant coursework on human- computer interaction and programming using modern web and mobile technology. Furthermore, I like to analyze and take inspiration from interfaces that have proven to be successful and consider these insights with my own work.

Outside of my studies, I enjoy playing tennis with my friends, watching soccer, and playing my favorite songs on the piano.
